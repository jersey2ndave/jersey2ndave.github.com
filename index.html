About Us

Jersey Films 2nd Avenue is Jake DeVito, Danny DeVito and Lucy DeVito's film and TV production company and the successor to Jersey Films.

It has a first-look deal with FX Networks for television projects, where it produced the 2022 animated series LITTLE DEMON, starring Danny, Lucy, Aubrey Plaza and Michael Shannon. Jersey also produced the Emmy nominated Amazon Original sports documentary series GAME 7 with hockey legend Mark Messier.

They've recently completed the feature film DRAG. A horror, comedy starring Lizzy Caplan, John Stamos and Lucy Devito which will premiere at SXSW 2026. Jersey is currently in development on a wide ranging slate of upcoming features, tv shows and online content in collaboration with filmmakers, various studios and production partners.
