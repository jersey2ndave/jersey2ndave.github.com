About Us

Jersey Films 2nd Avenue is Danny DeVito, Jake DeVito, and Lucy DeVito's film and TV production company and the successor to Jersey Films (GARDEN STATE, REALITY BITES, GATTACA, MAN ON THE MOON, HOW HIGH, PULP FICTION, OUT OF SIGHT, GET SHORTY, HOFFA, MATILDA, LIVING OUT LOUD AND ERIN BROCKOVICH).

It has a first-look deal with FX Networks for television projects, where it produced the 2022 animated series LITTLE DEMON, starring Danny, Lucy, Aubrey Plaza and Michael Shannon. Jersey produced the Amazon Original sports documentary series GAME 7 with hockey legend Mark Messier.

They've launched a new feature film initiative with DRAG. A horror, comedy starring Lizzy Caplan, John Stamos and Lucy Devito. Jersey is currently in development on a slate of upcoming features and tv shows and even a youtube series with various studios and partners.
